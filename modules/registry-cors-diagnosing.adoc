== Diagnosing CORS errors with browser developer tools

:_mod-docs-content-type: PROCEDURE

[role="_abstract"]
You can use your browser developer tools to identify the cause of CORS errors.

.Prerequisites

* {registry} is installed and running.

.Procedure

. Open your browser developer tools (F12).
. Go to the *Network* tab.
. Look for the failed request, which often shows as `(blocked:cors)` or has a red status.
. Look for a preceding `OPTIONS` request (the preflight) and inspect its response headers.
. Check whether `Access-Control-Allow-Origin` is present and matches your application's origin.
. Check whether `Access-Control-Allow-Methods` and `Access-Control-Allow-Headers` include the values your application needs.
